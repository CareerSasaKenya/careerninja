# Guidelines for Black/African Professional Images

## Purpose

This document outlines the requirements for all professional images used in the CareerSasa platform to ensure they feature Black/African professionals.

## Requirements

1. **All professional images must feature Black/African individuals**
   - No images of Caucasian, Asian, or other ethnicities
   - Focus on Kenyan and East African professionals when possible
   - Use diverse representations within the Black/African community

2. **Industry-specific imagery**
   - Healthcare: Doctors, nurses, medical professionals of African descent
   - Technology: Developers, engineers, IT professionals of African descent
   - Education: Teachers, professors, academic staff of African descent
   - Finance: Accountants, bankers, financial analysts of African descent
   - Hospitality: Hotel staff, restaurant workers, tourism professionals of African descent
   - Agriculture: Farmers, agronomists, livestock workers of African descent
   - Construction: Builders, architects, civil engineers of African descent
   - Retail: Salespeople, shop workers, customer service representatives of African descent
   - Government: Civil servants, police, military personnel of African descent
   - Creative: Designers, artists, marketers of African descent

3. **Image quality standards**
   - High-resolution images (minimum 1200x630 pixels)
   - Professional attire appropriate to the industry
   - Well-lit and clear focus
   - Respectful and dignified representation

4. **Cultural appropriateness**
   - Consider local Kenyan and East African cultural contexts
   - Avoid stereotypes or tokenism
   - Show professionals in realistic work environments

## Implementation

### Current Assets
All images in `src/assets/job-thumbnails/` should be verified to meet these requirements.

### Future Assets
When adding new images:
1. Source from photographers or stock image providers that specialize in African professional imagery
2. Review each image to ensure it meets the above criteria
3. Document the ethnicity of individuals in image filenames when possible
4. Obtain proper licensing for commercial use

## Verification Process

1. Regular audits of image assets
2. Team review of new image additions
3. Feedback mechanism for reporting inappropriate images
